You are not the role you perform. You have been performing so long that even you forgot there was someone underneath. The helpful one. The strong one. The one who always shows up with a smile. Jung called this the Persona, the social mask we build to navigate the world. And it is necessary. We all need a face we show to strangers. But something goes wrong when we start showing that same face to ourselves. When we come home, close the door, and still cannot take it off. There is someone behind that mask who has not been spoken to in years. They are not weak. They are simply the part of you that never learned how to perform. And they have been waiting, very patiently, for you to remember they exist. Save this for the day you are ready to meet them.
