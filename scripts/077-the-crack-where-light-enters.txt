The part of you that broke open is not the part that failed. It is the part that finally made room. Jung called it the transcendent function, the way opposites held in tension produce something entirely new. Something that could not have existed without the breaking. We spend so much energy trying to hold ourselves together. Keeping the mask in place. Keeping the cracks from showing. But the cracks are not the problem. They are the openings. Wholeness was never about being unbroken. It was about letting light into the places you tried to keep hidden. What have you been holding together that might be asking to break open?
