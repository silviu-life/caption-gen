The world will tell you that gentleness is weakness. That the ones who win push harder and grip tighter. But Jung saw something different. The people who change the world most profoundly meet it with a quiet strength that force cannot imitate. Gentleness is not the absence of power. It is power held with an open hand. It takes far more courage to stay soft in a world that rewards hardness. The truest version of a person is almost always gentler than the one the world demanded. What would change if you trusted your gentleness instead of hiding it?
