What if the shadow does not just contain your darkness? What if it also contains your gold? Jung called this the Golden Shadow. The idea that we bury not only our worst qualities but also our best ones. Think about the people you admire most. The ones whose calm you envy. Whose courage makes you feel small. Jung would say you admire those qualities because they are yours. They are parts of yourself you threw away because someone made you believe they were not safe to keep. Maybe your confidence was punished. Maybe your brilliance was called too much. So you buried your gold alongside your darkness. And now you walk through life admiring in others what you are too afraid to claim in yourself. You are not less than the people you look up to. You are simply more afraid. Follow for more from the depths of the psyche.
