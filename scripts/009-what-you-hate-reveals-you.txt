Why do certain people make your blood boil? Not mildly annoy you. But genuinely bother you in a way you cannot explain. Jung had a word for this. Projection. He said everything that irritates us about others can lead us to an understanding of ourselves. The person whose arrogance makes you furious might be showing you the ambition you were never allowed to express. The person whose neediness makes you recoil might carry the vulnerability you learned to crush in yourself. This does not mean you are just like them. It means they have activated something buried so long you no longer recognize it as your own. We do not project onto strangers. We project onto mirrors. The next time someone makes you irrationally angry, pause. Ask what you see in them that you refuse to see in yourself. What quality in others bothers you the most?
