The things you refuse to say out loud are running your life. Every boundary you swallowed. Every truth you traded for peace. Every no that came out as a smile. Jung understood that silence is not always golden. Sometimes silence is where the shadow hides. Because the words you will not speak do not disappear. They settle into your body. They become the tension in your jaw, the weight in your chest, the fog in your mind. And they control you far more than the words you actually say. What are you refusing to say that needs to be heard?
