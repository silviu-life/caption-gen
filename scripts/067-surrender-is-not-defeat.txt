We are taught that strength means holding on. That the strong person grips the hardest and controls the most. But there is a deeper power almost no one talks about. The open hand. Jung understood that the ego, our sense of control, is only a small part of who we are. Beneath it lives something larger he called the Self. And the relationship between ego and Self is not dominance. It is trust. Like a river that stops fighting its banks and discovers it was always being carried. You have felt this. A problem you stopped forcing that resolved itself. A fear that lost its power the instant you stopped running. That is the psyche finding its course. The question is not whether you can hold on. It is whether you are brave enough to let go. What are you gripping that might need an open hand?
