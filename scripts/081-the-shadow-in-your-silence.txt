The things you refuse to say are running your life. Every truth you swallowed to keep the peace became a stone in your chest. Every boundary you did not set became a wall between you and the people you love. Jung understood that silence is one of the Shadow's favorite hiding places. Not the silence of peace. The silence of fear. You learned early that certain words were dangerous. That honesty could cost you belonging. So you chose quiet. And the quiet kept you safe for a while. But it also kept you small. What you will not say out loud does not disappear. It speaks through your body, through resentment, through the distance you cannot explain. The shadow in your silence is not cruelty waiting to escape. It is truth waiting to be trusted. What have you been afraid to say out loud?
