There is a room inside your mind that you refuse to enter. You know exactly which one. You have walked past it a thousand times. You have locked it and posted a guard at the door. Jung would say that room contains your Shadow. Not a monster. Just the parts you decided were too painful or too frightening to look at. But here is what years of avoidance teach you. The longer that door stays shut, the more power whatever is behind it has over your life. It leaks into your relationships. It speaks through your anger. It shows up as the fog in your mind when you try to do the thing that matters most. The room does not need you to be brave. It needs you to be honest. Follow for more from the depths of the psyche.
