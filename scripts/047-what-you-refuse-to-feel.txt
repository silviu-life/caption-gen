Have you noticed the colleague who irritates you most is often doing something you secretly wish you could do. Speaking up in meetings. Saying no and meaning it. Carl Jung would say your office is a theater of projections. The coworker you call aggressive may carry the directness you were never allowed to show. The one you call lazy may reflect the rest you refuse to give yourself. We build professional masks just as we build personal ones. The shadow does not care whether you are at home or in a conference room. It will find a mirror wherever you go. Follow for more from the depths of the psyche.
