Some families run on invisible rules no one agreed to but everyone follows. Do not show weakness. Do not ask for what you need. Love must be earned. These rules are absorbed through a thousand small moments. A parent who withdrew when you cried. A home where only one emotion was allowed. Jung called these patterns complexes. Clusters of energy beneath awareness that quietly run your life. They choose your partners. They shape your reactions. We often repeat the very patterns we swore we would break. The child who hated being controlled becomes controlling. The one abandoned learns to leave first. Not because they are broken. Because the complex runs the old program. Breaking a generational pattern begins with seeing it. Just seeing it clearly and saying, this started before me. But I can choose differently. Follow if you are ready to break the cycle.
