You may have become exactly what you were told you would be. Not because it was true. But because a child will always choose belonging over truth. Jung observed that when a child hears a powerful figure define them, they do not resist. They adapt. If the message was you are difficult, the child becomes difficult. If it was you are too sensitive, the child buries their sensitivity and spends adulthood wondering why they feel numb. This is how a prophecy fulfills itself. Not through destiny. Through the quiet obedience of a child who needed love more than accuracy. And by the time you are old enough to question it, the prophecy no longer sounds like someone else's words. It sounds like who you are. You mistake the cage for your skin. But it was never your nature. It was an assignment given by someone who could not see you clearly. And the moment you truly see that, something in your chest begins to loosen. What were you told about yourself that you have been proving right your entire life?
