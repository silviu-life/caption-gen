Who was there for you during the worst of it? Not the people who said they would be. Who actually stayed? If you are honest, there may have been very few. So you learned to carry it alone. To talk yourself through the fear because no one else would. Jung called this part of individuation. The painful chapter where you realize the person you have been waiting for is you. Not because no one can love you. But because the foundation starts inside. You become the one who does not leave. Who says I am here even when the old fear says run. You may not have had someone who could hold that space. But you are becoming your own anchor. That is not loneliness. That is the beginning of something no one can take away. What would it mean to stop waiting and start staying for yourself?
