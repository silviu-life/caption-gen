If you keep having the same dream, it is not a glitch. It is a message you have not yet received. Jung understood that recurring dreams are the unconscious refusing to be ignored. The setting may shift. The details may change. But the feeling underneath stays the same. That is the thread. Not the images but the emotion they carry. The dream keeps returning because something inside you is still unfinished. A grief you skipped over. A truth you buried because it was too heavy to hold at the time. The unconscious does not forget. It waits with extraordinary patience. When you finally stop and ask what it wants, you often discover it was never trying to frighten you. It was trying to bring you home to a part of yourself you left behind. Follow for more from the depths of the psyche.
