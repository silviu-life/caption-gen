You are carrying things that do not belong to you. Some of what moves through you was set in motion before you were born. Jung understood that families pass down more than temperament. They pass down unfinished emotional business. The rage never expressed. The grief never allowed. The dream abandoned because survival came first. These things do not disappear. They sink into the patterns that shape the next generation without anyone choosing them. You may repeat a cycle you swore you would break. Choose the same relationship your parent chose. Fear the same things they feared. This is not weakness. This is inheritance. The first step toward freedom is recognizing that some of the weight on your shoulders was placed there by hands you never saw. You may be the first in your family brave enough to heal it.
