Why do so many capable people feel like frauds? Not the ones pretending. The ones who are genuinely talented but walk through life waiting to be found out. Jung would say this happens when there is a gap between your persona and your Self. The persona is the face you show the world. Not false. But incomplete. When people admire the mask and call it you, something inside knows. They are not seeing me. That recognition is impostor syndrome. It is not a thinking error. It is an accurate signal that the person receiving praise is not the whole person inside. The fraud feeling is not proof you are less than people think. It is proof you are more. The only cure is letting people see what lives behind the mask. Have you been waiting to be found out for who you are not, or who you actually are?
