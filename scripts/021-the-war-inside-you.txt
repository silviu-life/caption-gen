There is a war happening inside you right now. Not between good and evil. Between the person you show the world and the person you buried to survive. One is polished, appropriate, safe. The other is raw and terrifying in its aliveness. Jung understood this war is not a flaw. It is the central task of your life. He called it the confrontation with the Shadow. The longer you fight against yourself, the more exhausted you become. Not because you are weak. Because you are using half your energy to hold down the other half of who you are. The dark does not want to destroy the light. It wants to be included. And the moment you stop fighting and start listening, you do not become darker. You become whole. What would happen if you stopped fighting the part of yourself you are afraid of?
