Have you ever noticed that the same problems appear in every relationship you enter? Different faces. Same feeling underneath. Jung would say this is not coincidence. It is a mirror. Every outer relationship is shaped by the one you have with yourself. If you abandon yourself when you fall in love, you attract people who confirm that pattern. If you believe your needs are a burden, you choose partners who treat them that way. The pattern does not live in the other person. It lives in you. One of individuation's most painful truths is this. You cannot receive from someone else what you refuse to give yourself. When you tend to that inner relationship, something shifts in every outer one. Not because the world changes. Because you do. What would your relationships look like if you stopped leaving yourself to keep someone else?
