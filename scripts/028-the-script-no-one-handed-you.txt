You may be living an entire life that does not belong to you. Not because someone forced you into it. But because long ago, someone described who you were before you could describe yourself, and you believed them. Jung understood that children do not question the world they are born into. They absorb it. When a parent says you are selfish, or you only bring trouble, the child does not argue. They build their identity around those words. They spend decades proving the prophecy right without knowing they are doing it. And the cruelest part is this. The script no longer sounds like someone else's voice. It sounds like your own. It feels like common sense. It feels like realism. But it was never your truth. It was someone else's fear, or pain, or limitation, pressed into you like a fingerprint on wet clay.
