The strongest people you will ever meet are not the ones who learned to harden. They are the ones who stayed soft in a world that punished them for it. Jung spoke of integrating the Anima, the inner feminine that lives in every person. The capacity to feel without flinching. To remain open when everything in you wants to close. Force is borrowed strength. It comes from fear and leaves the moment the threat changes shape. Gentleness is a choice made from wholeness. A hand that stays open when the fist would be easier. Follow for more from the depths of the psyche.
