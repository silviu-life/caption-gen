Which version of you did you post today? Not the one who woke up tired, or scared, or unsure. The one with the right angle, the right words, the right life. Jung called this the Persona, the mask we build so the world will accept us. But here is the quiet danger. The mask was never meant to become your face. And yet, scroll by scroll, you are forgetting what you look like without it. The image you curate is not who you are. It is who you think they need you to be. And the distance between those two is where your loneliness lives. What mask did you put on this morning?
