Why do you chase love as if your life depends on it? Not because you are lonely. But because somewhere inside you, there is a half waiting for its other half to arrive. Jung called this the Anima in men and the Animus in women. The inner image of the missing piece. When someone resembles that image, something ancient wakes up. You do not just like them. You need them to be complete. But that missing piece was never in another person. It was in you. A part of yourself you disowned so long ago you forgot it was yours. You buried your softness because it was punished. Or your strength because it was unwelcome. And then you went searching for it in every lover who carried what you refused to carry yourself. What you are chasing is not a person. It is the part of you that you abandoned. Follow for more from the depths of the psyche.
