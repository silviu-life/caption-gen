Have you noticed that your relationships keep ending the same way? Different faces but the same story underneath. You give too much and they pull away. Or they come close and you disappear. Jung would say you are not choosing the wrong person. You are choosing the same wound dressed in different clothing. He called it projection. Your inner image of love, built in childhood from the first people who showed you what love looks like. If love looked like earning approval, you will be drawn to people who keep you proving. If love looked like distance, closeness will feel foreign. The psyche is trying to heal by recreating the original scene, hoping that this time you will respond differently. The pattern breaks not when you find the right person. It breaks when you finally see the pattern. Follow for more from the depths of the psyche.
