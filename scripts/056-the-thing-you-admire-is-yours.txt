What if the person you admire most is showing you who you already are. We talk about projection as if it only works with darkness. But Jung knew it works with light too. The calm you admire in your friend is not something they have and you lack. It is something you access in rare moments when you stop performing and let yourself be. The courage you see in someone who chased their dream is the very thing you buried the day someone told you your ambition was too much. Jung called this positive projection. We place our unclaimed gifts onto others and worship them from a distance, never realizing we are bowing to a reflection. Reclaiming your projections does not mean becoming arrogant. It means becoming honest. It means saying, that quality I see in them lives in me too. Like this and follow if the unconscious speaks to you.
