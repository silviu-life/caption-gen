Have you ever noticed how the most controlling people were once the most controlled. How the person who trusts no one once trusted completely. Jung had a name for this. Enantiodromia. The law that anything taken to its extreme flips into its opposite. The child never allowed to speak becomes the adult who cannot stop talking. The one who was smothered becomes the one who holds everyone at arm's length. We think we are choosing. But often we are living the mirror image of a wound we never processed. The pendulum swings because the psyche seeks balance. The way out is not to fight the swing. It is to find the still point between the extremes. Where you are neither reacting to the wound nor running from it. Just choosing from wholeness instead of pain. What opposite have you become without realizing it?
