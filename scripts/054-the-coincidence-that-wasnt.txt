You are thinking of someone you have not spoken to in years. That evening, they call. You find a book at exactly the moment its words answer the question you have been carrying. Jung called this synchronicity. A meaningful coincidence that cannot be explained by cause and effect. He suggested that there is a layer of reality where inner and outer are not as separate as we believe. When something inside you shifts, the world sometimes rearranges to match. Not because you willed it. Because the psyche and the world share a pattern deeper than the rational mind can see. We are trained to dismiss these moments. To call them luck or chance. But you know the feeling. The quiet shock of recognition. The sense that life was speaking directly to you. If this resonated, follow for more.
