Have you ever been deeply in love and then one morning woken up next to a stranger. Not because they changed. But because you finally saw them. Jung called this falling in love with the Anima or Animus. The inner image of the ideal partner, projected onto a real human being who never asked to play that role. In the beginning they carry your unlived qualities, your buried hopes. You do not see their flaws because you are not really seeing them. You are seeing your own reflection and calling it love. When reality breaks through, it feels like betrayal. But they did not betray you. The projection dissolved. And what remains is the real question. Can you love what is actually there? Not the dream. A whole, flawed human standing before you without the glow of your imagination. Real love begins not in the falling, but in the landing. Follow for more from the depths of the psyche.
